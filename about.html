What is Cryptocurrency?

Crypto, short for cryptocurrency, is a digital form of money that exists online. It enables near-instant transfers across borders and operates without a central authority when built on decentralized networks.

How it works

Cryptocurrency transactions are recorded on a blockchain, a tamper-resistant ledger. When enough transactions accumulate, they are grouped into a block and added to the chain after validating a secure hash. Different networks use consensus methods like Proof of Work or Proof of Stake to agree on the next block.

Why prices change

Price moves are driven by supply and demand, market sentiment, adoption, and liquidity. Since many cryptocurrencies have a capped supply, changes in demand can result in significant price movements.
